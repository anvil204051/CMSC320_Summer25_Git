
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git helps track changes in code, collaborate with others, and serves as a remote backup.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.
Answer: Identification and prediction of diseases.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?
Answer: Merging combines the histories of two branches without altering their commit order, while rebasing rewrites commits from one branch onto another for a cleaner, linear history.
